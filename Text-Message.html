Interview Simulator: Elevate Your Interview Skills, Land Your Dream Job.

Brief:

"Interview Simulator" aims to bridge this gap, providing job seekers with a comprehensive platform to enhance their interviewing skills, build confidence, and stand out from the competition.

Key Features:

Meet People - Realistic Mock Interviews with Random Pairing:

  • Dynamic Practice: Experience the unpredictability and spontaneity of real interviews through one-on-one video or chat mock sessions.
  • Diverse Exposure: Our random pairing algorithm ensures candidates interact with individuals from diverse backgrounds, cultures, and industries, thereby exposing them to a broad spectrum of interview styles and questions.
  • Constructive Feedback: Post-session, receive insightful feedback on performance, areas of improvement, and tips for refining your approach.

AI-Powered Interviews - Customized Prep for Targeted Success:

  • Resume Analysis: Simply upload your resume, and our cutting-edge algorithms dive deep to understand your background, skills, and experience.
  • Personalized Question Bank: Moving beyond generic questions, the AI crafts interview inquiries tailored specifically to your profile, ensuring you're never caught off-guard during real interviews.
  • Interactive Practice Platform: With the AI's guidance, practice your responses, refine your delivery, and get recommendations for showcasing your strengths effectively.
Built With

Node js, TensorFlow, Express js, OpenAi, PeerJS
