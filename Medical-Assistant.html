Medical Assistant for skin cancer

S

Skin cancer detection using AI.

Brief:

Skin cancer is a major public health problem, with over 5,000,000 newly diagnosed cases in the United States every year. Melanoma, the deadliest form of skin cancer, is responsible for an overwhelming majority of skin cancer deaths. In 2015, the global incidence of melanoma was estimated to be over 350,000 cases, with almost 60,000 deaths. Although the mortality rate is significant, melanoma survival exceeds 95% when detected early. To aid in early detection, we are developing a chatbot that helps diagnose melanoma using AI.

Our solution consists of two interfaces:

  1. Medical Assistant Chatbot: A chatbot that provides initial diagnosis guidance.
  2. Image Analysis Interface: Users can take an image of the affected skin spot, upload it, and the system will predict the probability of whether the condition could be skin cancer.
Built With

express.js, fastai, node js, render, uneeq
