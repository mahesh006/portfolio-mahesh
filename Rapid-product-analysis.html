Rapid product analysis

R

Social Media Monitoring powered by expert.ai.

Brief:

Managing a business is hard. So, we are trying to help small businesses and entrepreneurs control their brand and understand their products with the help of expert.ai by doing sentiment analysis across social media, reviews, and more. It is very helpful for small businesses to understand the feedback of their products. So, we built a tool to empower businesses to take control over their brand using expert.ai. We were able to make tools that can perform media monitoring and competitive analysis.

Features:

Search: We scrape data from Amazon, Yelp, Twitter, and the App Store, and sort reviews by sentiment. The results are displayed using user-friendly charts.

Sentiment Analysis: We have used the expert.ai API to segment sentiments into positive, negative, and neutral categories and assign sentiment to each mention.

Competitive Analysis: We build tools to allow small businesses to compare and overlay competitors' data with their own and understand what people are thinking about their product.

Built With

beautiful-soup, bootstrap, expert.ai, flask, heroku, sqlite, tweepy
